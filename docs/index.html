Project Objective

This project was developed to create an interactive online menu for a store specializing in handmade products. The main objective was to provide customers with an intuitive digital experience, allowing them to explore the products in a practical and visually pleasing way.

Main Features

⟶ Digital Catalog: Organized display of products with images, descriptions and prices.

⟶ Categories: Options for browsing products.

⟶ Product Details: Information on ingredients, available flavors and consumption suggestions.

⟶ Responsive Design: Compatibility with mobile devices and desktops for fluid navigation.

Project Benefits

⟶ Greater Reach and Visibility: Customers can access the menu from anywhere and learn about the products without having to go to the physical store.

⟶ Best User Experience: Intuitive interface that enhances products and facilitates navigation.

⟶ Business Highlight: A professional online menu conveys credibility and modernizes the store's digital presence.

Final Considerations

The development of this online menu demonstrates my ability to create customized digital solutions for small businesses. The project combines an attractive design with features that optimize the customer experience and drive sales.
